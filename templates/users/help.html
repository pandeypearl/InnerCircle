Groups

Broadcasts

Previewing Broadcasts

If you would like to preview what a broadcast will look like to your recipients you can do so by clicking on the recipient and view what the broadcast will look like for them.

Sending Broadcasts

A broadcast can be sent as soon as you create one or at a later stage.

To send a broadcast as soon as you create one just click the button.

To send a broadcast at a later stage just click on the button and you can send it at any time when clicking the button on that broadcast page.

Events

Previewing Event Invitations

If you would like to preview what a event invitation will look like to your guests you can do so by clicking on the guest and view what the invitation will look like for them.

Sending Event Invitations

An event invitation can be sent as soon as you create one or at a later stage.

To send an invitation as soon as you create one just click the button.

To send an invitation at a later stage just click on the button and you can send it at any time before the event date when clicking the button on that event page.

Event RSVPs

When guests RSVP to your event you will be notified on the Dashboard > Notifications page.

Lists

Sending Lists

A list can be sent as soon as you create one or at a later stage.

To send a list as soon as you create one just click the button.

To send a list at a later stage just click on the button and you can send it at any time when clicking the button on that list page.

List Checks

When list recipients check off items on your lists, you will be notified on the Dashboard > Notifications page.
