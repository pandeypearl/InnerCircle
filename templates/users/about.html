InnerCircle's Story

InnerCircle is a final project for the ALX's Software Engineering Plus with Backend Specialization Course.

This web application is a personal relationship tracker and organizer. It helps users keep track of the people that matter most and offers features that aid in making these relationships stronger.

The idea for the project transpired from a personal need to have all the people I care about in one place, like a contact book that also offers me a way to keep track of my interactions and feelings with the relationships that I find of most value. I also wanted a way in which to communicate important news and updates and have these stored all in one place. Additionally, I needed the application to fullfil the need to invite my community to large and intimate events, as well as communicate my preferences for things they might be interested in.

You can find out more about how I achieved this my creating your own account here or learn more about the application and its features here.
